21 years old tech geek from hungary, living and studying in norway. more about me

When I first visited Norway as an exchange student I had no idea I will take photography so seriously. But who you want to joke with... Norway showed me her thousand faces which I quickly fell in love with. There was no other choice anymore, that I had to preserve these memories for myself. So I started taking pictures. After 10 months in between these enormous mountains and vast, uninterrupted horizons of the ocean I was already caught by photography. But only when I went back to Hungary I realized that it is time to upgrade from ‘tourist photography’. After a year I moved back to Norway, and here I am now, searching for the perfect picture...
